1 People's emotions are always changeable and need to be dealt with rationally

Usually, when you talk to a person, you are fine, but suddenly one day, you use the same tone and expression, you will ignite the same person's temper and explode.

A person's temper is similar to all things with periodic rhythm (such as stocks and temperature) Similarly, when one is at the peak and the other is at the trough, there are different meanings for the analysis of the same sentence. When you are in a good mood, you can make a joke or discuss a controversial matter rationally. However, after any reason affects your mood, the joke becomes an insult, the discussion becomes an argument, and finally you blush and break up unhappily.

2 Programmer 'lying flat' is the most correct choice

Under the impact of the wave of 'inner curling', we see that many young people choose to lie flat to deal with the pressure. In fact, the two phenomena of 'inner curling' and 'lying flat' have always existed. Only recently, with the development of Internet information, people have noticed these and labeled them with these two vivid terms. Programmers are the first group to realize the inner volume, because from beginning to end, programmers need to maintain their learning ability in order not to be eliminated in the inner volume. If they feel heavy pressure, lying flat is the right choice.

4 What is the front end?

First define what is the front end. In a broad sense, any interface that can be seen by our human eyes and can conduct human-computer interaction belongs to the front-end category, such as app, website, applet, etc., and even the display screen on intelligent hardware (such as TV). From this point of view, as long as human-computer interaction is required, the front-end development will always be useful. I think you should also know that human-computer interaction is unlikely to disappear in a modern society with such developed science and technology, or in a short time.
